Darth Malt Milkshake

A cold thick nemiesis of the Jedi sweet tooth. The Darth Malt Milkshake is here to destroy the waistline of the Jedi order.

You'll need two hands to wield this frosty treat, follow these orders to serve your vengence thick and creamy.

  • yield: 4
  • prep time: 5 minutes
  • total time: 5 minutes

    • INGREDIENTS

    • 1 cup malt chocolate balls
    • 4 cups vanilla ice cream
    • 1/2-2/3 cup whole milk
    • 1/2 cup chocolate syrup
    • whipped cream

INSTRUCTIONS

In a heavy duty blender, add malt balls and chop into a fine powder. Add ice cream and 1/2 cup milk and process until combined and creamy. If it's too thick add a little milk and if it's too thin add more ice cream.

In tall parfait glass, swirl chocolate syrup around the glass. Gently pour milkshake into the center of the glass to keep the swirl in tact. Add a huge dollop of whipped cream, drizzle with more chocolate syrup and top with an extra malt ball or two.
